I found that It took too much time to write about a composer with not so much “original” contents. So now I prefer to putting the data on this page and let you people learn about the producers I adore by yourself (though no body would read my pages XD)
Today I am going to recommend PSYQUI. He is a music producer, working in MEGAREX which is known as a future-core club leaded by Lapix, another famous music producer. PSYQUI can provide really moving music, his melody is really charming.

This song Bye or not becomes popular upon uploading to the MUG SDVX (bemani), I truelly believe that this song can represent many personal styles of PSYQUI. More information is available in the link below.(Better be able to comprehend Japanese). https://qetic.jp/interview/psyqui-pickup/296527/
